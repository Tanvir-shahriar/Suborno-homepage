Mukta, a resilient middle-aged woman who has been navigating life with hearing impairment, once found it challenging to access even the most basic services independently. Everyday tasks that others might take for granted became hurdles, amplifying her sense of isolation. However, since discovering the Suborno app—a platform designed to bridge accessibility gaps—her life has transformed in remarkable ways.

Impact: Since discovering the Suborno app, Mukta's ability to access basic services independently has dramatically improved, empowering her with confidence and ease in daily life.
