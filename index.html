Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using our platform

The Suborno App is a digital platform created to support persons with disabilities in Bangladesh. It offers a wide range of features aimed at improving their lives by providing access to essential services.

The app is intended for users who are 13 years of age or older. For users younger than 13, parental or guardian supervision and consent are required. The primary users are persons with disabilities or individuals who support them.

Yes, the Suborno App offers various free services. This includes access to a free audiobook library , online courses and training , and information on free ambulance services.

The app is committed to protecting user privacy and personal data. It collects limited and necessary information to provide services , uses data strictly for matching users with services , and treats medical consultations with the highest level of confidentiality. Your information is never sold, rented, or shared with third-party advertisers.
