In-app Features

Suborno offers a comprehensive set of tools and services, all in one place, designed to support the everyday needs of people with disabilities. The app combines accessibility with practical support, making it easier to live independently and confidently.

Online Learning & Training

Take beginner-friendly courses on topics like Microsoft Office, freelancing, graphic design, and web development.

One-Tap Doctor Access

Instantly connect with qualified doctors from anywhere—no travel, no waiting. View profiles, book consultations, or call directly from the app.

Free Audiobooks Anytime

Browse a growing collection of Bangla motivational, educational, and entertaining audiobooks—perfect for visually impaired or reading-challenged users.

Accessible Transport Info

Need an ambulance or a safe ride? Find trusted free or low-cost transport options including app-based car rentals and emergency numbers—all in one place.

Create Your CV & Find Jobs

Step-by-step forms help you build a professional CV. Check job circulars specifically filtered for people with disabilities and get notified when new ones are posted.

Mental Health Help Is a Click Away

Access counseling centers, mental health helplines, and therapy booking—especially curated for emotional well-being and neurodiversity.

Legal Help & Complaint Support

If you’ve faced injustice or need legal support, easily submit a complaint through a guided form. We'll connect you with proper authorities or NGOs.

Find Assistive Devices

Discover where to get or request essential tools like wheelchairs, hearing aids, or educational devices—free or low-cost.
